Starrie

Scientific Name:

stella errans

Discovered by / at:

Victoria Lu, October 2025

Family:

stella. This is a newly discovered family and is named after Starrie.

Habitat:

In many magical unsuspected corners, and it turned out the most magical corner lies in the innocent corners of people's hearts!

Appearance:

Spinning little spirit with changing color and shape. The change of the shape is according to your interactions with it!

Key Characteristics:

  • Floats around.
  • Never stops spinning.
  • Changing shape and color.
  • Sleeps and wakes up.
  • Has a pair of beautiful wings that are usually hidden.

What we know so far about Starrie? Starrie is a cute little spirit that floats around and breathes all the time. It interacts with you by sometimes sleeping, sometimes awake or even flies. It never stops spinning and changing colors.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Try clicking your mouse, moving your mouse up and down, and then try the key codes "z", "x", and "c"!
